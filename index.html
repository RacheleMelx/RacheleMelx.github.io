About

I am a creative and curious person with a background in Architecture and getting ready to become a well equipped Service Designer. I have a strong passion for co-design and design-thinking processes. Last but not least I love getting my hands dirty.

Service Designer & Architect.

Here's a short story about me:
I was born in Tuscany 27 years ago. After the struggles of high school (ancient greek and latin weren't really my strong suit), I didn't give up on my education.
So, I decided to become an Architect, making the silent promise to myself to use my knowledge and capabilities acquired to help those in need.
Since then I spent my freetime filling up my University years with a great number of side activities, integrating my curricular experience with a deeper knowledge in fields related

to design and architecture.
Going from one workshop to another, my interests slowly shifted from architecture to self-building processes, then to design thinking and, finally, to service design. At the end, I was focusing more on the relations and interactions between people than the ones between buildings and architectural elements.
For this reason, after a short interniship experience as architect, I decided to move to Denmark to achieve a Master degree on the subject of Service Design.
So here I am now!
